Our Facilities

Eduford provides a magnificent and comprehensive range of academic facilities within the campus for its students and staff. A vast number of smart classrooms, lecture halls, tutorial halls and more are available on the campus. The classrooms at Eduford are contemporary, equipped with well-designed furniture, proper sitting arrangement, IT-enabled and LCD projector. The University has seminar rooms equipped with the latest audiovisual facilities which act as a common platform for students and faculty. The auditoriums and seminar rooms are extensively setup for regular interfaces, guest lectures, presentations, conferences, cultural events and other academic activities. The laboratories are well equipped with the latest equipments that students can use to perform their academic, practical and research activities. The central library of University is well equipped with a vast number of books/e-books, national/international journals/e-journals, magazines and newspapers for students. Eduford Business Incubation Foundation (IFTM-BIF) of Eduford is intends to create an ecosystem for promoting start-ups and innovations for students, faculty and alumni of Eduford by developing an end-to-end mentorship and incubation for potential business model with a motive is to build avenues of integration and partnership with other agencies. University offers preliminary health care facilities to all its students, teaching and non-teaching staff through own dispensary. The University has a Moot Court in which participants take part in a simulated court or arbitration proceedings, usually involving memoranda and oral argument. Museum, Herbal Garden and Business Lab facilities of University has given the opportunity to the students where they learnt the practical exposures.

Sports and Games are integral part of common life as well as important for good health. Sports help to reduce the levels of stress. Keeping this in view, University provides a proper training for the students to participate in sports programs under the Directorship of Dr. Vaibhav Trivedi. University also organizes fitness programs through gymnasium activities. Sports kits and equipments are available for the outdoor games facilities like Cricket, Football, Tennis, Basket Ball, Badminton etc. as well as for the indoor games like Table Tennis, Carom, Chess etc. Additional facilities are also available for cultural activities which are arranged for the students from time to time.
