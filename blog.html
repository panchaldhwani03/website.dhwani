Our Certificate & Online Programs for 2021

EduFord is also home to scholars from different part of the country. It aims to develop an environment which is focused towards the development of skills, knowledge and research to prepare the students for their future endeavours.


The strong Industry-academia linkages forged by Teerthanker Mahaveer University, and excellent job-oriented courses are tailored to meet the growing and ever-changing needs of the industry thus, drawing among some of the biggest corporate names across the nation. The recruitment drive has seen student’s participation from all departments and courses - Engineering, Management, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Arts, Agriculture, Law and sciences.


EduFord offers a potent interface between student and industry by organizing regular industry visits like recent visit to Bisleri, Yakult, Barclays, Coca Cola, Mother-Dairy and many more which helps students gain practical insight on manufacturing, operations, and management.


“An investment in knowledge pays the best interest” and making this their mantra, EduFord constantly focuses to improve and update themselves with the changing times and empower their students by facilitating mobility across disciplines as per their inclination, aptitude and at the same time fostering quality research.
